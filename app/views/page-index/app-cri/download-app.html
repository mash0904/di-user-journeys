Scan the QR code to download the GOV.UK ID Check app

How to scan a QR code
  1. Open the camera app on your phone.
  2. Position your camera so the QR code is visible on your screen.
  3. Wait until the QR code is recognised by your camera and a link appears on your screen.
  4. Tap the link to go to the App Store.
  5. Download the GOV.UK ID Check app.
How to download the app without a QR code
  1. Keep this browser window open.
  2. Open the App Store on your phone.
  3. Search for the 'GOV.UK ID Check' app.
  4. Tap the 'Get' button to download the app.
  5. When the app has been downloaded, tap 'Open' to use it.

You may need to use a second QR code to link the app

If the app asks you to do this, scan the QR code below.
